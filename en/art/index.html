Soundtrack album covers (since 2012)

(Re-)creating film soundtrack covers, for a strictly personal use, from official or non-official artworks. The aim is simply to have a cover that better represents the film, its score or that is more aesthetic and creative than a cover often design as an extension of promotional material rather than a work in its own right.

Depending on each cover, there can be more or less work to adapt it to a square image format. Sometimes, it's only a case of retouching an official artwork from the film's promotion, and other times, elements can be added to an image that needs to be modified. Analog photography (since 2021)

Using a point-and-shoot analog camera (Olympus Trip AF 30), with Kodak Gold 200 and Portra 400 (both 35mm) film, during my trips among others (below, pictures from Saint Julian's, Sliema and Buġibba in Malta, from Toulouse and Bordeaux in France and València in Spain).



Graphic Design class - Photography (June 2020)

Creating and experimenting with photography in a Graphic Design class at University of Malta (in Erasmus), of which the theme was "Transform the ordinary into the extraordinary". Using Adobe Photoshop and manipulating RGB channels, the idea was to extend the moment captured by a photograph, as well as the glimpse of identity represented by each self-portrait.
